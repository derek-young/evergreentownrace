RESULTS

Race results will be posted here live during the race. Participants must cross the the timing mats at the finish line with their bib number visible on the front and not covered by clothing. Results will only be edited (for mispelled names, etc.) for two weeks following the event. All results are based on gun time.

AWARDS

Awards will be based on gun-time for your event, and will be presented on the lawn in front of the lakehouse. *You must be present to pick up your award. Awards will not be mailed.

5k Awards

Awards ceremony will begin at 9:00am. 5k awards will be presented in the following categories:

  • Top-3 Overall Male & Female
  • 1st Place Male & Female Wheelchair
  • Top-3 Male & Female in the following age groups
    • - Age 12 & Under
    • - Ages 13-19
    • - Ages 20-29
    • - Ages 30-39
    • - Ages 40-49
    • - Ages 50-59
    • - Ages 60-69
    • - Ages 70-79
    • - Ages 80+

10k Awards

Awards ceremony will begin at 10:00am. 10k awards will be presented in the following categories:

  • Top-3 Overall Male & Female
  • 1st Place Male & Female Wheelchair
  • Top-3 Male & Female in the following age groups
    • - Age 19 & Under
    • - Ages 20-29
    • - Ages 30-39
    • - Ages 40-49
    • - Ages 50-59
    • - Ages 60-69
    • - Ages 70-79
    • - Ages 80+

Overall winners will be excluded from age group awards. You must be present to pick up your award, or have someone pick it up for you. Awards will not be mailed.
